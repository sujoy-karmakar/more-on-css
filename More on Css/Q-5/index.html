Google Fonts is a free, open-source project that provides a collection of high-quality web fonts that you can use on your websites. It offers a wide variety of fonts, making it easy for web developers and designers to enhance the typography of their web pages.

One of the popular fonts from Google Fonts is Montserrat. It is a versatile sans-serif font that is widely used for its clean and modern appearance. Montserrat is known for its readability and works well for both headings and body text.
